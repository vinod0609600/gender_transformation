## Notes
- Design focuses on trust, calmness, and professionalism using sage greens and soft blues.
- Typography uses 'DM Sans' for modern readability and 'Lora' for headings to add a touch of heritage/trust.
- Images will use Unsplash with strict medical/calm filtering (no graphic content).
- Form submission connects to POST /api/inquiries as defined in the schema.
